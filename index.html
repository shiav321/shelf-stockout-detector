01 — The Problem

Why I Built This

A real problem that costs the Indian retail industry billions every year — and nobody was solving it with AI.

⚠️ ₹50,000+ Lost Daily

One day I visited a DMart store in Hyderabad. I wanted to buy milk — the shelf was completely empty. I asked a staff member. They said "Sir, we'll restock in 2 hours." I left and bought it elsewhere.

That single interaction made me think: How many customers do this every day? How much money is the store losing? I researched and discovered:

🔴 Indian retail loses ₹2,000+ crores annually to out-of-stock (OOS) events. Staff manually walk aisles every 2 hours. They miss empty spots. It's too slow. Too expensive. Too unreliable.

💡 But every store already has CCTV cameras installed everywhere. The footage just sits there — unused. That's when I realized: I can make those cameras intelligent.

02 — The Solution

How It Works

A 5-step AI pipeline that turns existing CCTV footage into real-time shelf intelligence — no new hardware needed.

STEP 01
📹
CCTV Capture
Existing store CCTV cameras capture shelf footage every 30 seconds. No new hardware. No installation cost.
STEP 02
🔲
Zone Division
Each camera frame is divided into a 3×4 grid of zones — 12 zones per camera, each representing one shelf section.
STEP 03
🧠
AI Analysis
Each zone analyzed using texture variance + edge density (OpenCV) + CNN model (MobileNetV2) to classify: Stocked ✅ or Empty ❌.
STEP 04
🗺️
Store Map Update
A live store map updates instantly showing color-coded aisle status. Red = restock now. Green = fully stocked.
STEP 05
📧
Instant Alert
If 2+ zones are empty — email alert fired to store manager in under 30 seconds. Staff restocks before next customer arrives.
